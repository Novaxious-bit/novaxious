Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical kitchen renovation timeline?

A full kitchen renovation typically takes 6-12 weeks, depending on the complexity and material availability. We provide a detailed project schedule during the planning phase.

How much does bathroom remodeling cost?

Bathroom remodeling costs vary based on size and finish levels. Premium transformations typically range from $15,000 to $45,000+, including high-end fixtures and custom cabinetry.

How do you manage project duration?

We use dedicated project managers and a synchronized scheduling system to ensure all trades work efficiently and minimize downtime throughout the construction phase.

Is the initial consultation really free?

Yes, our initial design consultation is complimentary. We'll discuss your vision, provide expert advice, and outline a preliminary budget for your project.

What type of materials do you use?

We only source premium, commercial-grade materials from trusted luxury suppliers, including natural stone, architectural hardware, and designer cabinetry.

What kind of warranty do you provide?

We stand by our craftsmanship with a comprehensive 5-year structural warranty and manufacturer warranties on all installed premium fixtures. Our commitment to quality is absolute.
